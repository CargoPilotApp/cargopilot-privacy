Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 18, 2026

CargoPilot ("CargoPilot", "we", "us", or "our") provides a mobile application designed to help trucking and logistics professionals plan stops by sharing real-world tips and photos about locations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect your email address, a user identifier, and basic profile information you choose to provide.

User-Generated Content

When you submit tips or reviews, we collect the content you provide, including text, photos, selected locations, map coordinates, and timestamps.

Location Information

With your permission, CargoPilot may access your device location to show your position on the map, help find nearby locations, and improve the accuracy of location-based features.

Usage and Diagnostic Data

We may collect limited diagnostic and performance information to improve app reliability and user experience.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the CargoPilot app
  • To display community-submitted tips and photos
  • To support map and location-based features
  • To improve app performance and reliability
  • To protect users and prevent misuse

3. Sharing of Information

CargoPilot does not sell personal information. We may share data with trusted service providers that help operate the app (such as cloud hosting and authentication services), or when required by law.

4. Public Content

Tips and photos you submit may be visible to other users. Please avoid sharing sensitive or personal information in public content.

5. Data Retention

We retain information as long as necessary to provide the service and operate the app. Content may remain available unless removed or restricted for policy or safety reasons.

6. Your Choices

You can control permissions (location, camera, photos) through your device settings. You may request account or data deletion by contacting us.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system is completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

CargoPilot is intended for use by adults and logistics professionals and is not directed toward individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
